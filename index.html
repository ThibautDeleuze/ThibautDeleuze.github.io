Photogrammetry


These are the steps of a photogrametry I made on door in Bordeaux. It's a way to save heritage. To do ths I took many pictures of this door and from these data I build a cloud of dots and from this cloud a mesh. Then I cleaned the mesh with Zbrush. I like the idea of optimizing an automatic process with hand finishing.
