Augmented reality

This is an interactive poster that my team and I presented in a competition organized by the CMN, which is the public organization that rules national monuments in France. we didn't win this competition the goal was to promote Sauve-Majeure's abbey. We wanted to attract a young community by using social media interactions such as Instagram filters.

The idea was to show kind of a preview of the monument and we proposed the option of using this Instagram filter as a tour guide giving tourist information about this incredible place.

UX/UI

NPO (Nederlandse Publieke Omroep or Dutch Foundation for Public Broadcasting), asked us to redesign the recommendation system of their streaming platform. Here are a few samples of my prototype. We based our work on a data analysis, a user test and research papers about the topics we have dealt with (e.g. the swipable interface, user centric in recommendation systems, attention problematics).


We wanted to give the power to intuitively shape their digital environment to users who want to do so. We therefore assumed that the user should be informed of what the recommendation system thinks it knows about his or her tastes. And then that the user should be able to edit this information.
